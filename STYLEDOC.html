// 06 — non-negotiables

Brand Rules

01

Call content units by their names: Doc, Cheatsheet, Lab, Playground. Together they are The Stack — each one stands on its own.

02

Docs lead with generosity. Always free, always prominent. The first thing someone should find is the thing that helps them.

03

People choose their own depth. Start free, go as deep as you want. The product earns trust before it earns money.

04

REPPED is a social enterprise — tools and environmental supports built for real people. The technology is in service of the community, not the other way around.

05

Twitch is where people find us. It opens the door. The real work happens in the Stack, the Labs, and the community.

06

Language shapes experience. Write with precision and warmth. Words like "finally" and "actually" do more than jargon ever will.
